Nextech Squad

Nextech Squad is a vibrant community of tech enthusiasts and innovators . We are passionate about exploring the latest trends in technology, fostering creativity, and sharing knowledge among our members. Our mission is to create an inclusive environment where students can collaborate, learn, and grow together in the ever-evolving world of technology. Join us as we embark on exciting projects, workshops, and events that push the boundaries of innovation.
